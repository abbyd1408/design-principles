Continuity

"Our minds will continue a pattern beyond it's ending points. Further, our eyes will follow the direction of a line. On the left, our minds see a single cross shape insead of a four shorter converging lines. On the right, our eyes follow the direction of the A's swoop and continue on to the start. Applying this concept can add a sense of direction and movement to your layout. Ergo, flow." (White Space is Not Your Enemy, Pg. 62)

It's like looking at a rainbow of colors. If you start with a really bright red, and go from orange,to yellow, to darker colors, your eyes will naturally follow the nature of the pattern.
